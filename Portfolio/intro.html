❤ Project ❤

  Our project is the website called WildSnap, which was made by our team in the DECO7180 course. WildSnap is an innovative web application for wildlife enthusiasts, photographers, educators, and anyone curious about the wonders of nature. The platform allows users to share captivating wildlife photos, creating a space for nature lovers to come together and learn more about animals and their habitats.

  WildSnap helps users plan wildlife safaris. Through dynamic maps and trip plans, users can discover the best places and times to spot certain animals. Whether it's seasonal migration or specific animal sightings, our site provides valuable insights that make it easier for photographers, educators, and even nature lovers planning a trip to choose the right destination. Additionally, WildSnap provides users with the opportunity to learn more about animal behavior. By observing images and collecting information over time, users can learn how animals adapt to changing environments, such as different seasons or climates. This feature helps develop a deeper understanding of wildlife, making the WildSnap experience not only visually appealing but also educational.

  With its focus on sharing, learning, and exploring, WildSnap creates a space where people can appreciate the beauty of nature while contributing to a broader knowledge of wildlife.
